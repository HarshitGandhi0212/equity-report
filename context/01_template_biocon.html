▼ Risks

BEAR CASE
01Stretched valuation. 150× P/E with ROE of 4.68% — any earnings miss triggers sharp de-rating; bear targets ₹270–320 (−25 to −37%).
02US tariff overhang. 26% reciprocal tariff announced 02-Apr-2026 — direct impact limited but FII outflows and INR weakness create earnings headwind.
03FII unwinding. Holding down from 11.2% to 7.52% over 12M; DII absorption is masking the supply but pace is unsustainable.

05Competition intensifying. Samsung Bioepis, Sandoz, Celltrion, Pfizer all chasing same Denosumab / Aflibercept windows.
06Regulatory tail-risk. Any USFDA inspection observation (483, warning letter) on Bangalore / Vizag / Malaysia could derail margin trajectory.
07Capital efficiency drag. Goodwill from Biocon Biologics buyout (~₹13,000 Cr) suppresses ROE structurally for next 2–3 years.

The operational story is real — biosimilars at 26% EBITDA margin, integration complete, debt trajectory turning, multiple regulatory wins (Bosaya, Aukelso, Vevzuo, Micafungin in last 90 days). FY27 should see EPS rebuild toward ₹6.5–7.5 as the Lenalidomide base effect washes out and Denosumab US gathers volume.

But the entry price matters more than the story. At ₹425.75, the stock prices in nearly all of FY27's expected earnings recovery. Consensus 12M TP of ₹435 implies just +2.2% upside, while the bear case (ICICI Sec ₹320) flags −25% downside — asymmetric to the wrong side. ROE of 4.68% does not support a 150× P/E without faith-based extrapolation.

Tactical view: Existing holders → HOLD. Fresh money → WAIT for ₹350–370 zone (where 19.9% biosim industry CAGR + 21× EV/EBITDA gives genuine margin of safety). Aggressive entry only on biosim launch beat in Q1FY27 (Aug-2026 print) or US tariff resolution. Hard stop on positions: ₹305 (below 52W low + retest invalidation).
