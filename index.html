Methodology · Risk Score (0–100)

Each report computes a weighted composite risk score across three axes: Valuation (35%) covering P/E, P/B, EV/EBITDA, PEG, yield and Mcap/Sales benchmarked vs sector medians; Balance Sheet Health (35%) including debt/equity, interest coverage, working capital cycles, and asset quality; Growth Durability (30%) measuring revenue sustainability, market TAM, competitive moat, and management track record.

Scores above 60 = elevated risk. 40–60 = moderate. Below 40 = balanced risk-reward. The score is a starting framework for personal analysis, not a verdict — every report layers forensic notes, margin-of-safety checks, and downside scenarios.
